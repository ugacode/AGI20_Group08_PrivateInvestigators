What is Private Investigators?

Private Investigators is a concept for an asymmetric cooperative crime mystery game where players collaborate to solve a crime together as quickly as possible. Each player can play as one of several unique roles and together the players collect clues and use their deductive skills to make an accusation, revealing the guilty suspect and other details of the crime.

For this project we have created a technical demo showcasing two different roles, namely the City Rat and the Genius. The main idea behind these is enabling players to choose the role that most suits their preferences, by offering the possibility of playing their part entirely from home and thus in a Corona-safe environment if they wish to. Despite the unique features of each role, they are both equally fundamental to the progress of the game and interdependent. Only the combined effort of all the players will make the investigation around the mystery possible.
